The Goods We Trade

Areca Leaf Plates

Areca palm leaf plates are eco-friendly, biodegradable, and sustainable alternatives to plastic or paper plates. They are made from naturally fallen Areca palm leaves without cutting down trees. These plates are widely used for serving food at events, parties, and restaurants due to their sturdy and compostable nature.

Sugarcane Bagasse Plates

Sugarcane bagasse plates are eco-friendly, biodegradable, and compostable alternatives to plastic and Styrofoam plates. They are made from bagasse, the fibrous byproduct left after extracting juice from sugarcane. These plates are widely used in restaurants, catering, and food packaging due to their durability and sustainability.

Handicraft Products

Handicrafts are handmade decorative and functional items that reflect cultural heritage, artistry, and craftsmanship. They are made using traditional techniques, often with natural or locally sourced materials.

Poly Bags

Poly bags, or polyethylene bags, are flexible plastic bags used for packaging and storage. They are available in different types, sizes, and thicknesses, catering to various industries like retail, food, agriculture, and e-commerce.

Bamboo Products

Bamboo products are eco-friendly, sustainable, and biodegradable alternatives to plastic and wood-based items. Bamboo is a fast-growing, renewable resource widely used in home décor, kitchenware, furniture, and construction.

Coconut Products

Coconut products are widely used in food, cosmetics, and industrial applications due to their versatility and eco-friendliness. The global demand for coconut-based items is growing, especially in health, wellness, and sustainable product markets.

Pet Sheets

PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) sheets are thermoplastic polyester sheets known for their strength, transparency, and recyclability. They are widely used in packaging, thermoforming, printing, and industrial applications.
